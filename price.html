With many traditional businesses, sales is dependent on people who walk in to the store and make purchases.This is often limited by the location of the store and the hours at which the business operates
Ease of access to the internet and advances in technologies ie smartphones has increased the number of people able to access online services.This in turn allows them to access a myriad of services and products.
The internet provides your business an online audience.This is not limited by working hours and location of the customer or consumer as all services are offered through the internet.This convenience translates to not only better access to your products/services but also more sales
